We're open for the 2026 season.

After months of fleet prep, dock work, and route planning, Angler Watersports is officially live at **312 Talbot Street**, right in downtown Ocean City. Every jet ski and pontoon in the lineup is the latest model. Every guide on staff is Maryland-certified. Every safety briefing runs before the engine starts. No exceptions.

## What's on the Water

Two services, both pointed at the same goal: make the bay easy to enjoy.

- **Guided 1-hour jet ski tours.** Three riders per ski max, ages 5+. Brand-new fleet, certified lead, route built around wild horse habitat and dolphin territory.
- **Self-captained pontoons.** 2 to 8 hours, up to 10 guests, BYOB friendly. Anchor at sandbars, beach near Assateague's wild horse coastline, or just cruise at sunset. Pontoon reservations are by phone — call **(443) 664-6300** to book.

## What's Different

We're new. That's not a marketing line — it's the whole pitch. The fleet is fresh, the dock is downtown, the operating standards are written before guests show up. If you've rented from another OC company in the past five seasons, you'll feel the difference inside the first ten minutes.

## What's Next

We'll post here through the season — bay conditions, wildlife sightings, route notes, the occasional guest story. Bookmark the page or follow along when our socials are live.
